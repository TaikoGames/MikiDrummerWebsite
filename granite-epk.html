Bio

GRANITE is a Vancouver metal band that refuses to sit still in one genre. Equal parts crushing metal and dirt-under-the-fingernails grunge, their sound is heavy, hook-driven, and hard to file — the kind of noise that hits like a boot to the chest and lingers like a bad decision.

Forged in Vancouver's underground, GRANITE deal in downtuned riffs, raw-throated vocals, and a live show that leaves rooms sweat-soaked and ears ringing. It's grunge grit welded to metal weight: sludgy when it wants to be, savage when it needs to be, and always built to move a crowd.

Whether they're splitting a bill with local heavyweights or tearing up a basement show, GRANITE bring the same thing every night — real, loud, unfiltered heavy music from the Pacific Northwest. Hard to describe. Easy to feel.
