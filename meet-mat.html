Necalli Mat

A tribute build for Allegorithmic's Meet Mat challenge — Mat the robot, reimagined with Necalli's warpaint and hand-painted, battle-worn Substance textures.

Software — Substance Painter Type — Character / Prop Artist — Miki Butler

The build

"Meet Mat" is Allegorithmic's community art challenge, where artists take a single provided model — Mat the robot — and make it their own through texturing and rendering. This entry, Necalli Mat, wraps Mat in the tribal warpaint and glowing red eyes of Street Fighter's Necalli, finished with worn edges, chipped paint and grime for a real, played-with toy feel.
