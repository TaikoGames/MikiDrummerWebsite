Lift the Anchor

Punk Rock Vancouver, BC

Melodic, hook-driven punk rock out of Vancouver — big choruses, gang vocals, and songs about fresh starts and leaving things behind. For fans of Sum 41, Silverstein, Four Year Strong and Papa Roach.

Tracks: Hello RPM (Demo) · Aurora (Demo) · Cornered (Demo)

Granite

Metal / Grunge Vancouver, BC

Genre-bending metal and grunge from Vancouver, BC — downtuned riffs, raw-throated vocals, and a live show that leaves rooms sweat-soaked and ears ringing. Heavy, raw, and hard to describe.

Tracks: Blindsight · Skookum · Hemogoblin · Pony Up · Sour Diesel · Tiamat · Cursed · Astro Fungus · Devilbliss

Bound By None

Punk Rock Vancouver, BC

Bound By None is a Vancouver, BC punk rock band — fast, riff-driven songs with plenty of low end and hooks that stick. Tracks from the band feature on the Miki Drummer site player.

Diera

Punk Rock Barcelona, Spain

Diera is a punk rock band out of Barcelona, Spain — Spanish-language punk with speed, melody and bite. The Diera catalogue includes Bajo el nivel del mal, Groenlandia, La tragedia de los comunes and Sebastopol García.

Tracks: Bajo el nivel del mal · Groenlandia · La tragedia de los comunes · Sebastopol García
